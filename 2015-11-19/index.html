Google Analytics
for Web Developers

Or, the things you're not doing
but probably should be

Philip Walton / philipwalton.com / @philwalton

Question:

How many people here use Google Analytics?

How many of you do more than just copy and paste the JavaScript tracking snippet?

How did we get here?

Websites used to be so simple.
One request. One pageview. The End.

Now the web stack is extremely complex, and sites are more interactive than ever.

Yet most developers still just copy and past the same snippet, and only track pageviews.

The goals of this talk

I can't possibly teach you everything you need to know about Google Analytics in 30 minutes.

Instead, I hope to get you thinking, and inspire you to explore what's possible on your own.
